About this project
While walking home from school one day, six year old Andrew (nicknamed Ender) gets pushed around by the school bully. Ender was already beat up at home by his violent big brother Peter, so he attacked the boy. When he got home, a teacher from the battle school for future soldiers came to recrute him. He was taken to another planet not far away from earth were the battle school was. At the school every one is placed into teams for simulation games that simulate real battles in space. Ender was placed in the salamander team. As he grows up in the school, he quickly excels at the simulation and classes but fter a while he gets lonely. He still had good friends but could never keep them for more than a year because he was alsways was moved to classes for people his level. Even though he was still six he was put in a class for ten years old. He was also moved to a team here he was team leader of new recruits and had to train them himself. At 10 years old, he left the battle school entirely and went to a school for future generals. Most kids there were fourteen. He still was sad he lost contact with his family and the teachers worried this would affect his performance. They let him meet his sister Valentine once on earth, but that was the last time he visted Earth. At the new school for generals, Ender has to fight in simulations leading others to fight computer generated armies until he graduates.
