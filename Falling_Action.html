About this project
After the war Ender and his sister Valentine moved to another colonized planet that Earth now controlled. He traveled to other newer planets as a scout. On one planet, he found the last bugger still around in an egg, hidden. He learned that not all the not all the buggers where bad. He decided to help the bugger find a new planet to call home.
